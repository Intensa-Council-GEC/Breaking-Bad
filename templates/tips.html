Plan Your Meals

Plan your meals ahead of time to avoid overbuying and wasting food. Make a list of the ingredients you need and stick to it when you go grocery shopping.

Store Food Properly

Store your food properly to keep it fresh for longer. Use airtight containers for dry goods and wrap fruits and vegetables in paper towels to absorb moisture.

Get Creative with Leftovers

Don't let your leftovers go to waste. Get creative and repurpose them into new meals. For example, use leftover vegetables in a stir-fry or turn leftover chicken into a sandwich.
